Tending Sands Releases “Pressed”

— A Double Album of Edge, Elegance, and Electronic Catharsis —

Los Angeles, CA — November 22, 2025 — Independent artist and producer Jeramy Kendell Hoffman, known under his moniker Tending Sands, unveils his new album Pressed — an electrified alternative pop-electronic work that slices through modern static with intention, pulse, and purpose.

A two-part composition, Pressed is both confession and confrontation — a double-disc journey carved through introspection, synthesizer voltage, and emotional grit. It’s a project that refuses to pretend the darkness isn’t there, choosing instead to press straight into it.

“Pressed is me turning pressure into sound. It’s the beauty that leaks out when the walls are closing in — the light that survives the squeeze.”

Conceived, written, and mixed solely by Hoffman, the album marks a pivotal shift for Tending Sands — an artist who molds vulnerability into voltage. Each disc paints a different tension: the first atmospheric and reflective, the second urgent and defiant. Together, they form a push-pull evolution that mirrors Hoffman himself — creator, human, survivor.

Released under Shattered Hourglass LLC, Hoffman’s independent label, Pressed stands as both rebellion and rebirth — a declaration of autonomy in a digital world where algorithms try to smother the spirit.

With its haunting production, visceral lyricism, and emotionally raw soundscape, Pressed cements Tending Sands as a conscience inside modern alternative music — a ray of light cutting through the dark clouds that have followed Hoffman so far.

About Tending Sands

Tending Sands is the musical identity of Jeramy Kendell Hoffman — artist, producer, and founder of Shattered Hourglass LLC. His work braids alt-pop, electronica, and introspective narrative, exploring themes of identity, fate, censorship, and creative resistance. With Pressed, Hoffman pushes the boundary between sound and soul, inviting listeners to walk into the fire — and walk back out illuminated.

TENDING SANDS — THE PRESSED PRESS RELEASE

“Pressed” arrives like a flare in a blackout — an alt-pop electronic confession, produced and sculpted by Jeramy Kendell Hoffman. The record splits itself into two discs: one heartbeat, two chambers, both pumping a signal through the static.

This release marks a shift: sharper edges, deeper wounds, brighter sparks. It’s the kind of album that doesn’t ask for permission — it simply breathes on its own, and the world adjusts around it.

Tending Sands characteristically shapes commentary and catharsis into one continuous pressure wave — a mirror held to the darker corners of modern culture, yet unmistakably luminous in its defiance.

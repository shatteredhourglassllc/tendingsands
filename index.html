tending sands

Emerging from the arid landscapes of Apple Valley, California, Tending Sands—born Jeramy Kendell Hoffman on January 8, 1989—crafts music that mirrors the shifting sands of his environment. His compositions blend ambient textures, industrial rhythms, and experimental soundscapes, creating a genre-defying auditory experience.

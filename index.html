About

Tending Sands is a prophetic music project weaving together raw sound, layered textures, and defiant truth. Carving out space in a digital world bent on distortion, it resists silence with sharp edges, honest reflection, and sonic rebellion. Every track is both exile and return — a voice cutting through static.

tending sands

tending sands is a music project weaving together raw sound, layered textures, and defiant truth. carving out space in a digital world bent on distortion, it resists silence with sharp edges, honest reflection, and sonic rebellion. every track is both exile and return — a voice cutting through static. emerging from the arid landscapes of apple valley, california, tending sands—born jeramy kendell hoffman on january 8, 1989—crafts music that mirrors the shifting sands of his environment. his compositions blend ambient textures, industrial rhythms, and experimental soundscapes, creating a genre-defying auditory experience. tending sands’ discography is a testament to his prolific nature and thematic depth. albums like taking shape (2024) and making illusions (war is not real / reality of war is) (2024) delve into existential themes, while tracks such as “drop another bomb on a city” and “tear myself down” showcase his ability to intertwine political commentary with personal introspection. his music is available across various platforms, including apple music, amazon music, qobuz, spotify, and youtube, offering listeners a chance to explore his evolving soundscape. beyond his musical endeavors, hoffman operates under the label shattered hourglass llc, further emphasizing his commitment to producing and distributing his unique sound independently.

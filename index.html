Tending Sands

About

Emerging from the the arid landscapes of Apple Valley, California, Tending Sands—born Jeramy Kendell Hoffman on January 8, 1989—crafts music that mirrors the shifting sands of his environment. His compositions blend ambient textures, industrial rhythms, and experimental soundscapes, creating a genre-defying auditory experience.

Tending Sands’ discography is a testament to his prolific nature and thematic depth. Albums like Taking Shape (2024) and Making Illusions (War Is Not Real / Reality of War Is) (2024) delve into existential themes, while tracks such as “Drop Another Bomb on a City” and “Tear Myself Down” showcase his ability to intertwine political commentary with personal introspection.

His music is available across various platforms, including Apple Music, Amazon Music, and Qobuz, offering listeners a chance to explore his evolving soundscape.

Beyond his musical endeavors, Hoffman operates under the label Shattered Hourglass LLC, further emphasizing his commitment to producing and distributing his unique sound independently.

For those seeking a musical journey that challenges conventions and evokes the ephemeral nature of existence, Tending Sands offers a compelling and thought-provoking experience.

tending sands

Emerging from the arid landscapes of Apple Valley, California, Tending Sands—born Jeramy Kendell Hoffman on January 8, 1989—crafts music that mirrors the shifting sands of his environment. His compositions blend ambient textures, industrial rhythms, and experimental soundscapes, creating a genre-defying auditory experience. Tending Sands’ discography is a testament to his prolific nature and thematic depth. Albums like Taking Shape (2024) and Making Illusions (War Is Not Real / Reality of War Is) (2024) delve into existential themes, while tracks such as “Drop Another Bomb on a City” and “Tear Myself Down” showcase his ability to intertwine political commentary with personal introspection.

Jeramy Kendell Hoffman, performing as Tending Sands, is the founder of Shattered Hourglass LLC in Hesperia, California. Through his music, he explores prophecy, societal reflection, human introspection, and the hidden currents of culture. His work seeks to challenge, provoke, and illuminate, creating soundscapes that resonate with both urgency and subtlety. The Prophecy of an Atom and the Absence of Humanity delves into existential chaos, human absence, and the unseen threads that bind the world. This page showcases the album in full, inviting listeners to immerse in the intensity of his vision.
